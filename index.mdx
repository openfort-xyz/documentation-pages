## Why use Openfort? {{ anchor: false }}

Openfort is a wallet service for developers that want to offer a seemless experience to their players. It allows you to create and manage smart wallets powered by account abstraction.{{ className: 'lead' }}

- Openfort accounts offer flexbile ownership by design. When starting from a managed account, you can transfer the ownership of the accounts that hold the assets to external signers. All natively on the blockchain without having to export a private key.{{ className: 'lead' }}

- Openfort plugs-in your existing authentication infrastructure and is completly headless.{{ className: 'lead' }}

- Openfort accounts allow players to authorize and sign transactions without pop-ups, all happening locally on their front-end and securely.{{ className: 'lead' }}

- Openfort provides the headless wallet, you bring the rest of the stack.{{ className: 'lead' }}

- Openfort offers native programmable gasless transactions (and even ERC-20 based gas payments).{{ className: 'lead' }}
